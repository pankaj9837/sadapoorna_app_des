Title:
Sadapoorna Traders - Agent

FULL DESCRIPTION:
Sadapoorna Traders - Agent Portal is an internal enterprise management solution designed exclusively for authorized personnel, delivery agents, and system administrators to manage daily supply chain, accounting, and commodity trading workflows. 

Key Features & Internal Capabilities:
* Order & Credit Management: Track incoming customer orders, review historical credit approvals, manage due collections, and track cash reconciliation in real-time.
* Logistics & Dispatch Automation: Assign daily delivery routes to field associates, manage territory maps, update vehicle dispatch tallies, and optimize final-mile drop-offs.
* Warehouse & Stock Control: Live tracking of warehouse inventory levels (IN-OUT entries), stock adjustments, structural stock transfers, and quick stock reconciliation.
* Integrated QR Utilities: On-the-spot QR code generation for rapid inventory scanning, vehicle allocation, and warehouse verification checks.
* Financial & Analytics Oversight: Real-time generation of accounting records, voucher verifications (Journal/Payment), financial logging, and sales performance analytics.
* Workforce Administration: Manage secure internal employee lists, handle daily field attendance sign-offs, and process internal expense claim reports.
